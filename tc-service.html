Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a moderate complexity laboratory?

A moderate complexity laboratory is defined under CLIA regulations as a lab that performs tests categorized as moderate complexity by the FDA. These labs must meet specific requirements for personnel qualifications, quality systems, and regulatory compliance.

Why does my moderate complexity lab need a Technical Consultant?

It's a CLIA requirement. A Technical Consultant helps ensure your laboratory meets CLIA standards, maintains quality assurance, and stays inspection-ready. It also supports staff training, SOP development, and efficient workflows to improve accuracy and compliance.

How can consulting help with CLIA compliance?

Our consultants provide step-by-step guidance on CLIA regulations, including documentation, quality control, proficiency testing, and personnel competency assessments. We also prepare your lab for CLIA inspections and help maintain compliance year-round.

What areas of technical consulting do you cover?

We provide support in regulatory compliance, quality management systems, workflow optimization, instrument validation, staff training, and inspection readiness. Every consulting plan is customized to your lab’s needs.

How do I get started with your consulting services?

Simply contact us to schedule an initial consultation. We’ll evaluate your laboratory’s current operations, identify areas for improvement, and create a tailored plan to help your lab achieve compliance and efficiency.
